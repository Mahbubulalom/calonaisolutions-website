01 · WHAT IT DOES

What it actually does.

Your campaigns go out on time, on-brand, every sprint. The AI Campaign Manager takes a brief, a launch, a promotion, a content theme, and produces the full plan: subject lines, copy, visuals, send times, ad variants, landing copy. It schedules it, runs it, and reports back with what worked.

No more briefing agencies, no more campaigns slipping because someone's on holiday. It writes in your voice, pulls from your content library, respects your guidelines. When it's not sure, it asks. When it's confident, it ships.
