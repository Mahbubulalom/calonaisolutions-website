FEATURED · E-COMMERCE

A chatbot that actually reads the order history.

Customer service transformed for a mid-market UK e-commerce brand — 40% engagement lift, 35% CSAT increase, one less weekend support rota.

The bottleneck

Customer service was the single biggest cost centre that didn't earn anything. Six-person rota covering evenings and weekends. Response times slipped during sales. Repeat questions ate 60% of agent time. CSAT was flat at best.

What we built

  • AI triage chatbot reading real order history, not just a FAQ
  • Multilingual support across the markets the brand actually serves
  • ML-driven personalisation from past purchases & preferences
  • Seamless escalation: chatbot detects limits and hands off cleanly to a human
  • Slack & ticketing integration — no request falls through

Outcome, 60 days post-launch

  • +40% customer engagement
  • +35% CSAT
  • Routine inquiries automated — agents redeployed to complex cases
  • Higher conversion via personalised product recommendations
