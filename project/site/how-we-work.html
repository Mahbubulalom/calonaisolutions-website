How we work

One outcome. Three lanes to get you there.

A fixed-scope diagnostic, a fixed-price build, and an optional monthly partnership if you want one. No retainer before anything ships.

LANE 01 · WEEK 1

The Operational Diagnostic.

Four days inside your business. One document out the other end: the single system that, if built, would pay for itself fastest.

£4,000 fixed. Credited against the build if you go ahead.

We spend a week with your operators — not executives, operators. We ride the dispatch desk, read the inbox, open the CRM, shadow a job. We leave with a map of where your hours are going, and a single prioritised recommendation.

What you get

  • Bottleneck map — every process, scored for hour-cost per week
  • One priority system — the highest-leverage thing to build first, with the reasoning
  • Fixed quote — for the build, if you want one. No "it depends."
  • A 30-minute exec debrief — for you, your co-founder, your board. Delivered in plain English.
DayWhat happens
Day 1Kickoff. Meet the team. Tour the systems you already have.
Day 2Shadow your operators. Time the workflows. Read the emails.
Day 3Map the bottlenecks. Score by hour-cost per week.
Day 4Pick one. Quote the build. Deliver the debrief.

Diagnostic takes place onsite (UK) or remote. Most clients do a hybrid.

LANE 02 · WEEKS 2–5

The 30-Day Build.

Fixed scope, fixed price, weekly working demos. We build the system. Your team learns to own it.

From £12,000. Priced and quoted before we start.

The build is four weeks because most "AI projects" drag out because nobody wrote down the scope. We write the scope. We quote it. If it's going to take more, we'll tell you before we start — not in month eight.

Weekly rhythm

  • Monday — sprint plan, shared with you
  • Wednesday — mid-week check-in (15 min, optional)
  • Friday — working demo. Not slides. The thing.
WeekMilestone
Week 2System skeleton + integrations to your existing tools
Week 3Core logic. Test with real data from last month.
Week 4Staff-facing UI. Handover training starts.
Week 5Go-live. Shadow mode → live. 30-day measurement begins.
LANE 03 · MONTH 2+

The Operator Partnership.

Optional. A light monthly engagement to keep the system sharp and spot the next bottleneck worth fixing.

From £2,400 / month. 30-day rolling. Cancel any time.

Most clients take this for six months and then graduate. Some don't — they run the system with their in-house team and call us when they want the next one. Either is fine.

What's included

  • Monthly operational review — what the system did, what it missed
  • Tuning, prompt adjustments, minor extensions
  • Quarterly "next bottleneck" diagnostic (your whole business, not just the system)
  • Priority response for incidents and questions
Honest note

We'd rather you didn't need this.

The systems we build are designed to run themselves. If you don't need the partnership, don't buy it. We'd rather lose recurring revenue than sell you something that's already doing its job.
