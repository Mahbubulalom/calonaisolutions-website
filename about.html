About

A consultancy that would rather ship fewer things, better.

Calon is a UK-registered AI consultancy. We work with founders and operators who are tired of the slide-deck version of transformation and want the Friday-afternoon version.

What we believe

Three convictions.

They shape how we quote, what we build, and who we say no to.

01 — AI is a tool, not a strategy.

If the answer is a form, a Zapier flow and a shared calendar — we'll tell you. We'd rather lose the build than sell you the wrong one.

02 — Operators, not executives, design the system.

We spend our discovery week with the people who do the work — not the people who describe it in meetings. The difference is where most AI projects fail.

03 — Ship in 30 days or don't start.

A year-long AI transformation is a year-long way to avoid the decision. We compress. It keeps everyone honest.
