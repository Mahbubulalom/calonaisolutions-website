02 · AI REACTIVATE MEMBERS

Win back the ones
who already said yes.

An AI worker that scores your dormant members, predicts who's most likely to return, and runs the reactivation sprint across email, SMS and call — so revenue you already earned doesn't walk out the door.

Book a chat > See what we've shipped
01 — WHAT IT DOES

What it actually does.

It finds the 20% of lapsed members driving 80% of your recoverable revenue — then goes and gets them. The model ranks every dormant contact by likelihood to return, picks the right channel for each (email, SMS, or a callback queue for your team), and runs the sequence — personalised to why they left and what brings them back.

At the end of each sprint you get a clean report: who came back, who's warming, who to drop. Every sprint gets smarter as the model learns which messages, timing and offers actually move people.
